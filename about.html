LEAH LUX MASCHER

b. 2002 Cape Town

Leah Lux Mascher is an artist based in Cape Town. She works in drawing, installation, video, performance and photography. She graduated in 2024 from Michaelis School of Fine Art. She received a Simon Gerson prize for her graduate exhibition presentation. Mascher has participated in a number of group exhibitions in Cape Town, as well as performative presentations under the alias "Lux".
